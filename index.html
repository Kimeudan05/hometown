Welcome to Nairobi, Kenya

Explore some of the places you can visit in this beautiful country!

Top 10 places You Can visit in Nairobi

Nairobi National Park

Experience wildlife just outside Nairobi city with lions, giraffes, and more.

Giraffe Centre

Feed and interact with the endangered Rothschild giraffes.

David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust

Watch adorable orphaned elephants being fed and playing in the mud.

Karen Blixen Museum

Learn about the life of author Karen Blixen and Kenya's colonial history.

Nairobi Railway Museum

Discover the history of rail travel in Kenya through vintage trains and exhibits.

Bomas of Kenya

Enjoy traditional music and dance performances from Kenya's diverse cultures.

Kazuri Beads Tour

See how hand-made beads and pottery are made and buy unique souvenirs.

Nairobi National Museum

Explore Kenya’s rich history and culture through exhibits on archaeology and art.

Ngong Hills

Hike and enjoy stunning views of Nairobi and the Great Rift Valley.

Westgate Shopping Mall

Shop, dine, and enjoy entertainment at one of Nairobi’s top malls.

Lake Naivasha

Enjoy a boat ride on Lake Naivasha, a freshwater lake surrounded by stunning scenery, and spot hippos and birdlife.

Mau Mau Caves

Explore the historic Mau Mau Caves, which were used by freedom fighters during Kenya's struggle for independence.

About Nairobi

Nairobi is the bustling capital city of Kenya, known for its unique combination of wildlife and urban development. From the Nairobi National Park to a thriving tech scene, Nairobi is an exciting place to explore.
